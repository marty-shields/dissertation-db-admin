Overview

Welcome to the Database Administration Tool. This tool is for students at the University of Sunderland in order to aid students who work with databases. Use this tool in order to make backups of your databases that you use for tutorials and assignments for your modules. You can also see what changes you have made to your datbases over time and use that information if required for a tutorial or assisngment.


Use the side bar on the dashbord page to view what databases you currently have connected and to view your changes which you have made for your databses. There is also a menu bar at the top where you can access database and profile settings.
